Due to Corona Virus outbreak and impact of global economics crisis all the industries are shutting down so due to this Students are facing lots of problems and they are in under pressure. If We talk about B.Tech Students they are facing so many issues and 3rd Year students are not getting Internship and they has a fear of placement preparation also. So, some Institutes are giving better opportunity to students to utilize this time period of quarantine and learn some new skills. We are a part of one such Best Programming Institute ‘Coding Ninjas’.

We are glad to be a part of Coding Ninjas Ambassador Program because they are helping a lot to students to utilize this lock down period. They organised an event ‘Ninja Thon’ in which students in a group of Team have to make a website. So ,we ‘Team Cheese Weasels’ came up with an idea of making such website which can helps other in this pandemic situation. As people have to search a lot regarding any COVID facilities or issues, so we tried to club all the facilities in a single platform.
